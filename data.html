Data Visualisation & Storytelling Challenge

“ Data is everything. ” “Data is the new gold. “ ....

But wait, what do they actually mean?

As I embark on my journey in demystifying the new “gold rush” of data science, I took on this challenge to unearth how professionals utilise data in the business environment and decision-making processes. That’s not only it! It was also an opportunity to test my hands at converting data to meaningful stories - also an opportunity to reveal the creative side of me!

Insights

We were given the flexibility to decide on a specific topic as the given theme - 'Ecommerce' is broad and general. In order to narrow down the theme and accurately frame a problem, we hopped on a “Data Discover Trip '' and underwent a week of relentless research, collecting, wrangling and analysing data. As a result, we discovered patterns and similarities in our data - that is the Ecommerce industry has started to personalise customer experience by integrating gamification features, live chat functions and data analytics.

But now, the question is how can we convey these insights in a creative manner to grab the attention of the judges? Our answer is an animated and engaging video - as if you’re watching a cartoon! Hope you enjoyed the video!

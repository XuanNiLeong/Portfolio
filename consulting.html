Insights

As a student consultant for a Digital Health, BioInformatics startup in the UK, I assisted my client in their expansion to Asia by identifying, researching and classifying the multifaceted variables present in the healthcare and technology industries. My area of research focuses on data privacy ( ‘impact of Covid-19 on data privacy’, ‘consumer perception towards data privacy’ ) as part of the team's effort in identifying potential expansion opportunities for their Fully Homomorphic Encryption Technology. After several rounds of team discussions and client meetings, my team and I uncovered that the Japanese greatly value the importance of data privacy and that the Covid-19 pandemic has welcomed threats to biopharma companies in Japan. As a result, I proposed to partner with drug development companies in Japan in order to reduce the risk of being targeted by hackers.
